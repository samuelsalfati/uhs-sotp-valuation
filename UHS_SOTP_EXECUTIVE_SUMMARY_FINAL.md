### Why Sum-of-the-Parts (SOTP)?

Traditional EV/EBITDA valuation **understates value** for asset-heavy healthcare operators because:

1. **Owned facilities have no rent expense** ‚Üí EBITDA appears higher but includes implicit real estate return
2. **Market applies blended multiple** ‚Üí Ignores that behavioral health deserves premium vs acute care
3. **Real estate not marked to market** ‚Üí Book value ($6.6B) significantly understates market value

**Definition**: Rate of return on a real estate investment based on the income the property generates.

```
Formula:  Cap Rate = NOI / Property Value

```

**Example for UHS PropCo**:

**What Cap Rate Represents**:
- **Unlevered return** on the real estate asset
- Similar to earnings yield (inverse of P/E ratio)
- Lower cap rate = Higher property value (and vice versa)
- Market-determined based on property type, location, quality, tenant credit
